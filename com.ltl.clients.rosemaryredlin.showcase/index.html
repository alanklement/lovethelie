Rosemary Redlin Makeup Artist

Internationally renowned makeup stylist Rosemary Redlin fuses her creative intuition and sense of glamour in editorial work which has graced the pages of Harper’s Bazaar, Marie Claire UK, Rolling Stone, Playboy,Vive, Easy Living and British GQ.

Rosemary’s work has also hit the runways where she was key artist for designers like Maria Pinto, Lyn Devon, and Heike Jarick. Her creative hand has been called upon by celebrity clientele including Ginnifer Goodwin, Bradley Cooper, Busy Phillips, Rosie O’Donnell, and Megan Fox. Rosemary has also worked on the industry’s top TV and print commercials in campaigns for Chevrolet, Converse, Dillard’s, Polo, and Rolex. Rosemary believes that “beauty should begin from within, that a woman’s inner beauty is her truth.”

She brings out the intrinsic beauty in every subject; her dedication to her craft combined with her incredible talent enhances each individual face. Rosemary’s constant thirst and love for art of all mediums influences all of her beauty looks.Rosemary Redlin currently resides in New York City and is a member of Local 798.
